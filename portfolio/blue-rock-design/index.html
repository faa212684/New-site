Glendon Lambert ties are made by hand in the heart of Brooklyn, New York. The ties are created from the finest silk, wool, cotton, and linen, using traditional techniques and working with some of the industry’s best mills. Taking inspiration from the heirloom quality ties, we created an identity and color palette that feels true to the brand—timeless, sophisticated, with punches of bold color. The branding includes logo, stationery, packaging, marketing materials, and tag collateral. To order your custom Glendon Lambert tie, check out their website here. Designed by Blue Rock Design.
