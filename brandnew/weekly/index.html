About

Brand New × Weekly is published every Monday and serves as the weekly newsletter for the blog Brand New, providing a day-by-day summary of content posted across its three categories — Reviewed, Noted, and Linked — from the preceding week. Collecting the cumulative data of the polls for each post and selecting some of the most memorable reader comments, Brand New × Weekly offers subscribers an additional way of enjoying the blog’s content and appreciating their fellow readers’ contributions.
